Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

1. Who We Are

KritSystems provides Discord-related services, Discord automation, custom bots, ticket systems, server builds and support tools.

This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, store and protect personal information when you use our website, Discord servers, bots, support tickets or services.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Discord username, display name and user ID.
  • Discord server name, server ID, channel IDs or role IDs where needed for bot functions.
  • Messages or information submitted through tickets, forms or support requests.
  • Project requirements, examples, screenshots, links or files you choose to provide.
  • Payment or billing notes, where relevant to a service.
  • Technical information needed to operate bots, logs, commands or support systems.

3. Why We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide Discord setup, bot development, support and maintenance services.
  • Create, manage and respond to tickets or enquiries.
  • Configure Discord bots, roles, channels, permissions and panels.
  • Track project progress, delivery records and support history.
  • Protect our services from abuse, spam, scams, unsafe activity or misuse.
  • Improve our bots, services, documentation and support processes.

4. Lawful Basis

Where UK data protection law applies, our lawful basis may include contract, legitimate interests, legal obligations or consent, depending on the situation.

For example, we may need certain information to provide a requested service, manage a support ticket, protect our systems, or keep reasonable business records.

5. How Long We Keep Information

We only keep personal information for as long as reasonably needed for the purpose it was collected, including service delivery, support, records, security, legal or business reasons.

Ticket logs, delivery records, client notes and support records may be kept while a project, service relationship or maintenance arrangement is active, and for a reasonable period afterwards.

6. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be processed through third-party platforms we use to provide our services, such as Discord, GitHub, hosting providers, payment providers or other tools needed for support, development or delivery.

7. Discord Bots and Logs

KritSystems bots may process Discord IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, command usage, ticket records, setup records, delivery records or moderation/support logs where needed for bot functionality.

Bot logs are used for operation, troubleshooting, abuse prevention, support and service records.

8. Cookies and Analytics

This basic GitHub Pages website does not intentionally use tracking cookies. If analytics, forms, embeds or third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated to explain what is used and why.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse or disclosure. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location and the law that applies, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the use of your personal information.
